<p><p>The cow cheese market consists of two main types: natural cheese and process cheese. Natural cheese is made through traditional methods using milk, cultures, and enzymes, without additives, resulting in distinct flavors and textures. In contrast, process cheese combines natural cheese with emulsifiers and other ingredients for enhanced shelf life and uniformity, often offering a milder taste and easier melting properties. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary applications, contributing to the overall growth of the cheese market.</p></p>
