<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) Recovery and Abatement Market includes various technologies for reducing VOC emissions. Regenerative Thermal Oxidation utilizes heat to oxidize VOCs, while Recuperative Thermal Oxidation recovers heat for energy efficiency. Catalytic Oxidation employs catalysts to lower reaction temperatures. Photo-oxidation uses UV light to transform VOCs, and Adsorption by Activated Carbon captures them on porous materials. Rotor Concentrators increase VOC concentration before treatment, and Cryocondensation involves cooling to condense VOCs for recovery or disposal. Each method varies in efficiency, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
