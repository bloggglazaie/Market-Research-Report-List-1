<p><p>Exchangeable tip drill markets are categorized into three main types: straight shank, taper shank, and others. Straight shank drills have a uniform diameter throughout their length, allowing for easy clamping and versatility in standard applications. Taper shank drills feature a conical end, enhancing grip in the drill holder and providing better torque transfer, ideal for heavy-duty usage. The "others" category encompasses specialized designs, such as screw shank or custom-configured drills, catering to niche applications and specific industry needs.</p></p>
