<p><p>The inventory robots market comprises two primary types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics involves autonomous robots that navigate dynamic environments, efficiently transporting goods within warehouses or retail spaces. These robots adapt to changing layouts and improve operational flexibility. In contrast, fixed robotics refers to stationary systems, such as conveyor belts and automated guided vehicles, that operate in designated areas, optimizing the inventory management process. Together, these technologies enhance efficiency, accuracy, and productivity in logistics and inventory management.</p></p>
