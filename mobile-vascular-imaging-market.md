<p><p>The mobile vascular imaging market encompasses various imaging modalities that aid in diagnosing vascular conditions. X-ray imaging provides quick visualization of blood vessels but involves radiation. Ultrasound offers real-time imaging without radiation, utilizing sound waves to assess blood flow. CT scans deliver detailed cross-sectional images, enhancing diagnostic accuracy but also use radiation. MRI uses magnetic fields for high-resolution images of soft tissues, offering excellent visualization of blood vessels. Nuclear imaging systems utilize radioisotopes to assess blood flow and perfusion, particularly in cardiac evaluations.</p></p>
