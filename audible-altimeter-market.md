<p><p>The Audible Altimeter market can be divided into two primary types: those with electronic screens and those without. Audible altimeters with electronic screens offer digital displays that provide easy-to-read altitude information, often including other features like pressure settings and memory functions. In contrast, audible altimeters without electronic screens rely on mechanical components or simple auditory signals to indicate altitude changes, making them more straightforward and durable, but lacking detailed visual data. Both types cater to differing user preferences and needs in altitude measurement.</p></p>
