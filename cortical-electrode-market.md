<p><p>The cortical electrode market primarily features two types: strip electrodes and grid electrodes. Strip electrodes are linear arrays designed to facilitate targeted brain stimulation or recording along a specific region, making them ideal for less invasive procedures. In contrast, grid electrodes consist of a two-dimensional arrangement, allowing for broader coverage and detailed mapping of cortical activity. Both types are crucial for neurological research and clinical applications, such as epilepsy monitoring and brain-computer interface development, enhancing understanding of cerebral functions.</p></p>
