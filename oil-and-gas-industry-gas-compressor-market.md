<p><strong>The Oil and Gas Industry Gas Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement</li><li>Dynamic Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas industry utilizes two primary types of gas compressors: positive displacement and dynamic displacement. Positive displacement compressors function by trapping a specific volume of gas and reducing its volume, effectively increasing pressure. Examples include rotary and reciprocating compressors. In contrast, dynamic displacement compressors rely on high-speed rotating elements to impart velocity to the gas, converting kinetic energy into pressure. Centrifugal and axial compressors are common examples. Both types play crucial roles in gas processing, transportation, and storage.</p></p>
