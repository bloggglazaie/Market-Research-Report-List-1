<p><strong>The High Speed Photodetector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Detector</li><li>Thermal Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed photodetector market is primarily divided into two types: photon detectors and thermal detectors. Photon detectors, such as avalanche photodiodes and photomultiplier tubes, operate by converting light into electrical signals, offering rapid response times ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, thermal detectors sense changes in temperature caused by incident light, providing slower response times but higher sensitivity for low-power detection. Both types serve diverse applications, including telecommunications, medical imaging, and industrial sensing, catering to varying speed and sensitivity requirements.</p></p>
