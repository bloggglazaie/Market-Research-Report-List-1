<p><p>The smartphone gamepad market is primarily categorized into two types: bracket-mounted and clip-mounted devices. Bracket-mounted gamepads securely attach to the smartphone, providing stability and enhanced control similar to traditional handheld consoles. Both types aim to improve the gaming experience by enhancing grip, reducing hand fatigue, and facilitating a more immersive gameplay environment for mobile gamers.</p></p>
