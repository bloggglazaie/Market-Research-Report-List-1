<p><p>The cast resin dry-type transformers market is primarily divided into two main types: AN (Naturally-Cooled) and AF (Forced-air-Cooled). AN transformers rely on natural convection for cooling, making them ideal for indoor applications with lower heat loads. In contrast, AF transformers use external fans to enhance air circulation, allowing for better heat dissipation and higher loading capacities. Both types are valued for their safety, minimal maintenance, and environmental resilience, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
