<p><p>The radiation shielding systems market is segmented into two main types: indirectly ionizing and directly ionizing radiation. Indirectly ionizing radiation, such as gamma rays and x-rays, requires shielding materials like lead, concrete, or specialized polymers to prevent exposure. Directly ionizing radiation, including alpha and beta particles, necessitates different approaches, often involving denser materials or specific barriers to safeguard against energetic particles. Both market types address safety regulations and the need for protection in medical, industrial, and nuclear applications.</p></p>
