<p><strong>The Organic LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pmoled</li><li>Amoled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic LED market is primarily divided into two types: PMOLED and AMOLED. PMOLED, or Passive Matrix OLED, utilizes a simple structure, making it ideal for smaller displays like wearables and simple screens. In contrast, AMOLED, or Active Matrix OLED, features a more complex design with a backplane that allows for faster refresh rates and higher resolutions, making it suitable for smartphones, televisions, and large displays. Both types leverage organic compounds for vibrant colors and energy efficiency.</p></p>
