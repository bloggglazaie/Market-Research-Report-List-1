<p><p>The caulking tools market comprises three main types: manual, air, and electric caulking tools. Manual caulking tools offer a simple, hands-on approach for applying sealants and are favored for smaller projects. Air caulking tools utilize compressed air for efficient application, catering to larger and more demanding tasks. Electric caulking tools provide powered precision, allowing for quicker and more consistent bead application, making them ideal for professional use and extensive projects. Each type serves distinct needs within the construction and DIY sectors.</p></p>
