<p><p>The medium hydro turbine runner market encompasses various types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and efficient, suitable for medium-to-high head applications. Kaplan turbines excel in low-head scenarios, utilizing adjustable blades for optimal performance. Pelton turbines are ideal for high-head, low-flow conditions, employing impulse action for energy extraction. Other turbine types may include crossflow or bulb turbines, each designed for specific site conditions and operational needs, collectively driving the growth in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
