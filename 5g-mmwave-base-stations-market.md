<p><p>The 5G mmWave base station market includes "Integrated Small Cell" and "Distributed Small Cell" types. Integrated Small Cells combine radio and antenna elements into a single unit, facilitating easier installation and deployment in dense urban environments. In contrast, Distributed Small Cells consist of separate radio units connected to antennas, allowing for greater flexibility in placement and better coverage over larger areas. Both types aim to enhance network capacity, improve user experience, and support the growing demand for high-speed connectivity.</p></p>
