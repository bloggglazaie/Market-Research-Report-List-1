<p><p>The synthetic dimethicone market is categorized by viscosity levels: low, medium, and high. Low viscosity dimethicone offers a lightweight texture, making it ideal for formulations like lotions and serums. Medium viscosity dimethicone strikes a balance, providing both spreadability and a silky finish, suitable for creams and hair products. High viscosity dimethicone is thick, creating a robust barrier; it’s often used in products needing enhanced moisture retention and lasting effects, like ointments and specialized skin treatments.</p></p>
