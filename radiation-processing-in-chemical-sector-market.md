<p><p>Radiation processing in the chemical sector involves using ionizing radiation to modify materials and enhance their properties. The market is divided into two main types: radioisotope-based systems, which utilize isotopes like cobalt-60 for irradiation, and electronic accelerators, which generate high-energy electrons for similar purposes. Radioisotope systems are often preferred for their simplicity and effectiveness in applications such as sterilization, while electronic accelerators provide faster processing speeds and greater control, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
