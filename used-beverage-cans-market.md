<p><p>The used beverage cans market consists of two primary types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate metal components—top, bottom, and body—usually welded together, offering robust strength. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single sheet of metal, forming a seamless body and bottom, providing a lighter and more efficient design. Both types are recycled extensively, contributing to sustainability efforts in the beverage industry and helping reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
