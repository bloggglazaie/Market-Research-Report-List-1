<p><p>Medical EHR software tools are primarily categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access from anywhere with internet connectivity, promoting flexibility and reducing upfront costs. Conversely, on-premise EHR solutions are installed locally on healthcare providers' servers, offering tighter control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
