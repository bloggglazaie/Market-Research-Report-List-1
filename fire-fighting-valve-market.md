<p><p>The fire-fighting valve market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions in fire protection systems. Butterfly valves enable quick operation and are space-efficient, while UL/FM gate valves provide reliable shut-off. Pressure reducing valves manage system pressure for optimal performance, and alarm check valves signal issues with water flow. Deluge valves release large water volumes for rapid suppression, while ball valves offer tight sealing and quick on/off control. Gate valves are essential for isolating sections of a system, with other types catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
