<p><p>Sweep generators are classified into several market types based on their operational characteristics. Glide sweep generators provide a continuous frequency change, ensuring smooth transitions across a range of frequencies. Stepped sweep generators operate with discrete frequency increments, allowing for precise control and measurement at specific intervals. Time sweep generators vary frequency over a defined time period, making them suitable for time-domain analyses. Other types may include advanced configurations or hybrids, catering to niche applications in various testing and measurement scenarios.</p></p>
