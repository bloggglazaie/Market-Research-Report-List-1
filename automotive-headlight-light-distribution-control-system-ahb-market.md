<p><p>The Automotive Headlight Light Distribution Control System (AHB) enhances vehicle safety by improving visibility during night driving. It includes types such as the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), which adjusts the headlight angle based on steering input, ensuring optimal illumination around curves. The Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB) automatically adjusts the intensity and shape of the light beam, providing improved visibility while preventing glare for oncoming drivers. Both systems are crucial for enhancing road safety and driving comfort.</p></p>
