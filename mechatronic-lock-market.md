<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Contactless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechatronic lock market is divided into two main types: contact type and contactless type. Contact type locks require physical interaction, using mechanical components in conjunction with electronic systems that enable functionalities like key authentication or biometrics. In contrast, contactless type locks leverage technologies such as RFID or NFC, allowing users to unlock doors without direct physical contact, enhancing convenience and security. Both types cater to different user preferences, applications, and security requirements in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
