<p><p>Activated carbon for mercury control is categorized into three primary types: powdered, granular, and others. Powdered activated carbon (PAC) is finely ground, enhancing its surface area, making it effective for quick adsorption of mercury in air and water treatments. Granular activated carbon (GAC) features larger particles, offering longer contact time for more sustained mercury removal, suitable for industrial applications. The "others" category includes specialty forms like extruded or felted carbon, designed for specific environmental conditions and tailored applications in mercury control.</p></p>
