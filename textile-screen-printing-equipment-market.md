<p><p>The textile screen printing equipment market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual equipment relies on human intervention for each printing step, offering low production rates but high customization. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process with some automated features, improving efficiency while still requiring operator input. Fully automatic systems are designed for high-volume production, with minimal human intervention, maximizing speed and consistency. Each type caters to different production needs and budget considerations in the textile industry.</p></p>
