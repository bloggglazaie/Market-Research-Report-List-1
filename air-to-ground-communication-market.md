<p><p>Air to Ground Communication encompasses two main market types: Web Via Satellite using Ku Band and Terrestrial Mobile Services, including 3G and 4G. Web Via Satellite leverages high-frequency satellite technology (Ku Band) to provide internet access to aircraft, facilitating real-time data exchange and passenger connectivity. Conversely, Terrestrial Mobile Services utilize ground-based cellular networks to offer communication; 3G and 4G provide varying degrees of speed and coverage, ensuring reliable connectivity both in-flight and during ground operations.</p></p>
