<p><p>The Medical X-Ray Computer Tomography (CT) scanners market features three main types: single-detector CT, double-detector CT, and multidetector CT. Single-detector CT uses one detector for imaging, offering basic functionality. Double-detector CT enhances imaging speed and quality with two detectors. Multidetector CT, commonly used in modern medical imaging, utilizes multiple detector rows, providing high-resolution images rapidly, reducing scan times, and improving patient comfort. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs, influencing their adoption in healthcare settings.</p></p>
