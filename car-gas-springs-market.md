<p><ul><li>Lift Gas Spring (Non-locking)</li><li>Lockable Gas Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car gas springs market is primarily divided into two types: lift gas springs (non-locking) and lockable gas springs. Lift gas springs provide smooth lifting and support for car hoods, trunks, and tailgates without a locking mechanism, allowing for easy operation and adjustability. In contrast, lockable gas springs offer the added feature of securing the lifted position, making them ideal for applications requiring stability and safety, such as in hatchbacks or service doors that need to remain open under various conditions.</p></p>
