<p><ul><li>Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD)</li><li>Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiation Hardened Electronics market is primarily categorized into two types: Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD) and Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP). RHBD focuses on designing electronic components with inherent resilience to radiation effects, employing techniques such as redundancy and error correction to ensure reliability. In contrast, RHBP involves modifying manufacturing processes and materials to enhance the radiation tolerance of components, often targeting semiconductor fabrication methods. Both approaches aim to ensure functionality in high-radiation environments, such as space and military applications.</p></p>
